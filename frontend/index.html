// About SignBridge

Making ASL visible to everyone

SignBridge is an open-source platform that harnesses the power of computer vision and machine learning to recognize and translate American Sign Language in real time.

Our Django backend processes each video frame through a trained CNN model, detecting hand landmarks and predicting the corresponding ASL letter — all within milliseconds.

Whether you're Deaf, hard of hearing, a student learning ASL, or building more inclusive tools, SignBridge gives you instant, accurate translation through just a webcam.

// Features

Everything you need
to communicate freely

Built for speed, accuracy, and accessibility from the ground up.

⚡

Real-Time Detection

Frame-by-frame analysis delivers predictions under 50ms, powered by an optimized CNN running in Django with OpenCV preprocessing.

🧠

Deep Learning Model

Trained on thousands of labeled ASL images, our model accurately distinguishes all 26 alphabet letters plus common phrases.

🔒

Privacy First

Video is processed locally in your browser — only compressed frames are sent to the server. No footage is stored or recorded.

📝

Sentence Builder

Consecutive predictions are assembled into words and sentences in real-time, with smart debouncing to eliminate duplicates.

🎯

Confidence Scores

Each prediction includes a confidence percentage and top-5 alternatives, letting you verify or override the result instantly.

🌐

Open Source

SignBridge is fully open source. Fork it, extend it to more sign languages, or integrate it into your own accessibility tools.

// How It Works

Four steps from gesture to text

01

Open Camera

Press Space to activate your webcam. No downloads. No plugins. Works in any modern browser.

›
02

Show Your Hand

Position your hand in the frame and form ASL letters. The green bounding box guides your placement.

›
03

Django Predicts

Frames are sent to the Django API, processed by the CNN model, and a prediction returns in milliseconds.

›
04

Read the Text

Detected letters build into words and sentences. Copy, speak, or share your translated message.
